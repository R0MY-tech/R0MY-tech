### [Career Conductor — UX/UI Design Project](https://www.figma.com/design/tmIc8m7CIOgrKsCzb3Gejf/Career-Conductor?node-id=0-1&t=wYLIAW0l796W037p-1)
> A Figma prototype designed to help high schoolers discover the right college major for them, focused on both visual design and ease of use.

![Figma](https://img.shields.io/badge/Figma-444?style=flat-square&logo=figma&logoColor=white)
![UX/UI](https://img.shields.io/badge/UX/UI_Design-444?style=flat-square&logoColor=white)
![Research](https://img.shields.io/badge/User_Research-444?style=flat-square&logoColor=white)

<details>
<summary>📖 Read more...</summary>

<br/>

**The Problem:** High schoolers often struggle to pick a college major due to frustration, lack of information about college requirements, or limited resources — leading to wasted time if they choose incorrectly. This is supported by data: in a survey of approximately 690,000 students aged 15–16 from over 80 countries, about 4 in 10 students reported being unsure about their career expectations.

**The Solution:** An app that helps students discover their major and learn about the college requirements associated with it.

**Our Process:**
- Conducted interviews with middle schoolers and high schoolers to collect real insights
- Created wireframes to plan the layout and functionality of the app before design
- Built a full interactive Figma prototype prioritizing clean design and smooth user experience

This project was completed as part of the **RWC UX/UI Design program** at NJIT.

</details>

🔗 [View the Figma Prototype](https://www.figma.com/design/tmIc8m7CIOgrKsCzb3Gejf/Career-Conductor?node-id=0-1&t=wYLIAW0l796W037p-1)
